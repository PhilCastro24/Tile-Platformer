
# **Tile-Platformer**

You step into the shoes of a brave adventurer exploring vibrant and ***treacherous landscapes.*** 
Leap across platforms, dodge ***hazards,*** and ***defeat enemies*** as you make your way through each ***challenging level.*** 
Collect ***coins*** and ***power-ups*** to boost your score and ***stay alive!***

---

### **Gameplay Features**

- **⚔️ Classic Platformer Action**  
  ***Jump, run,*** and ***fight*** through levels filled with ***enemies and obstacles.***
  Use your ***agility*** and ***quick reflexes*** to navigate the ***dangers*** that await.

- **💰 Collectibles and Power-ups**  
  Find and collect ***coins*** to increase your ***score,*** and look for ***unique power-ups.***
  Each collectible adds to the excitement and your progress!

- **🧩 Dynamic Environments**  
  Explore levels designed with a ***mix of puzzles, platforming challenges,*** and ***enemy encounters.***

- **📈 Increasing Difficulty**  
 As you progress, hazards grow ***more complex.***
 Each level will test your skills and push you to improve as a platformer champion.

---

### **Core Mechanics**

- **🎮 Smooth Player Controls**  
  With ***responsive, precise controls,*** jump and run through obstacles effortlessly.

- **💀 Enemy Encounters**  
  Enemies like ***skeletons*** roam the levels, ***challenging*** you at every step.

- **🏆 Scoring and Lives System**  
  ***Collect coins*** and earn points while keeping an eye on your remaining lives .
